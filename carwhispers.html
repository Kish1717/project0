Volkswagen T-Roc

Volkswagen India has launched the T-Roc in India and has priced it at Rs. 19.99 lakh (ex-showroom India). This of course is the introductory price of the car. The Volkswagen T-Roc is part of the brand's new product offensive and its second launch this month after the Tiguan AllSpace.
Land-Rover Defender

Land Rover has launched the all-new Defender in India with prices starting at Rs. 69.99 lakh for the base Defender 90 variant, going all the way up to Rs. 86.27 lakh for the range topping Defender 110 First-Edition variant (all prices ex-showroom, India).
Mercedes-Benz GLE

Mercedes-Benz has finally launched the all-new GLE SUV in India at a starting price of Rs. 73.70 lakh for the 300 d, going all the way up to Rs. 1.25 crore for the range-topping 400 d Hip Hop variant, (all prices ex-showroom, India).
Land-Rover Discovery Sport

The New-Gen Land Rover Discovery Sport comes with sharper design and styling cues with a lot of black detailing. While the grille design remains unchanged it is now all-black and flanked by a sleeker set of LED headlamps with integrated LED daytime running lamps.
KIA Carnival

The Kia Carnival is the second offering from the South Korean carmaker in India and enters a new growing segment of full-size luxury MPVs. The Carnival officially went on sale in India at the Auto Expo 2020, and it’s offered in 3 variants - Premium, Prestige and Limousine and it comes in 7-, 8-, and 9-seater cabin layout options.
Mercedes-Benz GLC

The Mercedes-Benz GLC Coupe was launched in India on March 3, 2020, and it’s the first non-AMG Coupe SUV from the carmaker in India. While the Coupe-SUV has come to India for the first time, globally this is the facelift version of the SUV that was unveiled last year.
