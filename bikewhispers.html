Ducati Diavel 1260

The new generation Ducati Diavel 1260 was been launched in India at a price of Rs. 17.7 lakh for the standard variant, while the Diavel 1260 S is priced at Rs. 19.25 lakh (ex-showroom, India). The Diavel has been a standout model for Ducati even since it was launched and the power cruiser has gained a cult following of sorts.
Jawa Perak

The new Jawa Perak keeps the bobber design intact with the low-slung and floating single seat with integrated taillight, teardrop fuel tank and a low-set and wide handlebar. The pillion seat is optional on the motorcycle.
BMW R 1250RT

The BMW R 1250 RT is a touring motorcycle and it too gets subtle updates in terms of colours used. The motorcycle gets a dual-tone colour scheme of Blue Planet Metallic and Sparkling Storm Metallic.
Bajaj Dominor 250

Bajaj Dominar 250 motorcycle was launched in India last month at Rs. 1.60 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). Bajaj Auto introduced the baby Dominar Intending to emulate the success achieved by the bigger sibling, Dominar 400.
Triumph Rocket 3R

British motorcycle maker, Triumph Motorcycles has introduced the new generation Rocket 3 in India. The 2020 Triumph Rocket 3 made its global debut in 2019 and has quickly made its way to India at a price tag of Rs. 18 lakh (ex-showroom).
